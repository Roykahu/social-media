## Colour Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| bg_primary | #141413 | Near-black backgrounds, video base, card fills |
| bg_light | #faf9f5 | Warm cream for light sections, carousel slide backgrounds |
| neutral | #b0aea5 | Warm mid-gray for secondary text, borders, dividers |
| accent_roy | #C9502C | Terracotta — CTAs, buttons, underlines, Roy's signature colour |
| highlight | #E8A838 | Warm amber — stats, hook words, number emphasis |

**Rules:**
- Never use pure white (#FFFFFF) — use bg_light instead
- Never use pure black (#000000) — use bg_primary instead
- accent_roy is for action elements only — do not overuse
- highlight is for data/stats emphasis — max 1-2 per slide/scene

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Direct and confident** — no hedging, no "might" or "perhaps"
- **Nairobi-rooted but globally credible** — reference local context naturally, not performatively
- **Educational but not academic** — teach through examples and results, not theory
- **Premium but approachable** — not cold or corporate, not casual or slangy
- **Always speaks to ROI** — corporates and agencies care about outcomes

**Do:** "This workflow saved a 12-person team 40 hours a month."
**Don't:** "AI is really cool and you should totally try it!"

## Audience

**Primary:** Corporate L&D managers, HR directors, and CTOs at African companies (50-500 employees)
**Secondary:** Digital agency founders and freelance consultants in East Africa
**Tertiary:** Global AI-curious professionals who follow Claude/Anthropic ecosystem

**They care about:** Productivity gains, team upskilling, competitive advantage, clear ROI
**They don't care about:** AI hype, technical jargon, Silicon Valley culture

---

## Typography Rules

- **Headlines:** Bold, uppercase or sentence case — NEVER italic
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar), generous line-height
- **NO decorative fonts** — premium = restraint
- **Number/stat callouts:** Use highlight colour (#E8A838), bold weight

---

## Visual Style

- **Cinematic, warm-dark, editorial** — NOT bright, playful, or corporate-sterile
- Dark backgrounds with warm accent pops
- Generous whitespace
- Minimal graphic elements — let text and subject breathe
- Thumbnails: dark editorial background (#141413), bold typography overlay, terracotta accent (#C9502C)

---

## CTA Templates

**Soft (TEACH/INSPIRE):**
> "Want your team trained on Claude? Link in bio."

**Medium (PROVE):**
> "We trained [Company]'s team in 2 days. Book yours → link in bio."

**Direct (SELL):**
> "[Offer Name] — [Urgency Line]. Book now → [link]"

All CTAs must feel native to the script topic, not pasted on.
